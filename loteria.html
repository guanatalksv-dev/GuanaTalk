How to play Guanaco Lottery?

What is it about?
The system will display a definition or clue of a typical Salvadoran slang word ("caliche") on the top white screen.

How to win:
1. Read the English clue carefully.
2. Find the matching Salvadoran word card on your board and click it.
3. If you are correct, a neon tropical marker will drop on top of it.
4. The game has 5 consecutive rounds. Complete each block to unlock brand new local words!
