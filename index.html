ABOUT US

GuanaTalk began as an initiative aimed at enhancing the tourist experience in El Salvador by facilitating comprehension of the local language and promoting knowledge of the country's traditions. Over time, the project evolved into an educational platform focused on cultural dissemination.

How does it work?

GuanaTalk offers a structured learning experience in three stages:

Explore

Access content about Salvadoran expressions, culture, gastronomy, and relevant facts about the country.

Learn

Understand the meaning of each word, expression, or tradition through clear explanations and practical examples.

Practice

Reinforce your knowledge through interactive activities such as educational games and learning exercises.
