Did you know?

The National Shield features a vibrant landscape with five volcanoes, symbolizing the five original states of the Central American Isthmus. The rainbow above represents peace and hope.

National Tree

Did you know?

The Maquilishuat is El Salvador's national tree. In the dry season, it loses its leaves and bursts into beautiful, dense pink blossoms, painting the landscape with color.
