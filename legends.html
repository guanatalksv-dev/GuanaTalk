Legends

Discover tradittional tales filled with mistery, culture and imagination


La Siguanaba

A ghostly woman who appears near rivers at night to lure men with her beauty, only to reveal a terrifying face when they approach.

El Cipitio

A mischievous boy with a straw hat who loves eating corn and playing tricks on people; he symbolizes innocence and folklore humor.

La Carreta Chillona

A haunted cart that roams the streets at night, screeching loudly as a warning of death or misfortune approaching.
