ABOUT US

GuanaTalk began as an initiative aimed at enhancing the tourist experience in El Salvador by facilitating comprehension of the local language and promoting knowledge of the country's traditions. Over time, the project evolved into an educational platform focused on cultural dissemination.

How does it work?

GuanaTalk offers a structured learning experience in three stages:

Explore

Access content about Salvadoran expressions, culture, gastronomy, and relevant facts about the country.

Learn

Understand the meaning of each word, expression, or tradition through clear explanations and practical examples.

Practice

Reinforce your knowledge through interactive activities such as educational games and learning exercises.

Color psychology of our website

Our color palette was carefully selected to represent the identity, culture and values of El Salvador.

Blue

It symbolizes the sky and the ocean that surround us, unity, and the flag of our country.

Sky blue

It represents rivers, lakes and beaches, tranquility and harmony with nature.

White

It represents the purity and peace of our people, and the warmth and hospitality that characterizes us.

Green

It represents our mountains, life, lush nature, and hope for growth and renewal.

Gold

It represents the beauty of our sunrises, sunsets and fertile lands.

Brown

It symbolizes our history, traditions and cultural richness.

Pink

It symbolizes love, solidarity and commitment of our people.
